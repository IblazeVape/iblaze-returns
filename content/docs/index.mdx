Reflow gives your Shopify store a branded, self-serve returns portal. Instead of
customers emailing you or hunting through Shopify's default account pages,
they land on a portal that looks like *your* store, find their order in
seconds, and start a return themselves.

## What Reflow does — and doesn't do

It's important to be clear about this up front: **Reflow does not approve or
decline returns.** That decision always happens in Shopify, exactly as it
does today. Reflow only replaces the customer-facing part of the flow — the
part where a customer finds their order and requests a return — with a
portal you control the look and rules of.

Think of it as a better front door, not a new backend.

## What you can configure

Everything below is available from your Reflow admin, no code required:

- **Return window** — set anywhere from 1 to 90 days from delivery.
- **Sidebar navigation** — show, hide, or relink any item in the customer
  sidebar (News & Updates, Returns Policy, Speak to Support, and so on).
- **Brand colours** — carries through to the customer avatar and accent
  colours across the portal.
- **Returns policy checkbox** — turn the "Accept our returns policy" step on
  or off, and edit exactly what it says.
- **Button colours** — recolour the Submit, Accept, and Review buttons to
  match your storefront.
