---
title: FAQ
description: Common questions from merchants setting up Reflow.
---

### Does Reflow approve or decline returns?

No. Shopify still makes that decision, exactly as it does today. Reflow
only gives your customers a branded, self-serve place to start a return —
everything downstream still runs through Shopify.

### Do I need to touch my theme?

No. Reflow doesn't modify your storefront theme or product catalogue. You
install the app, and link to your portal from wherever makes sense (order
confirmation emails, your storefront footer, account page).

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes, on paid plans. Point a subdomain like `returns.yourstore.com` at your
portal — see [Customization](/docs/customization#custom-domain).

### What happens to orders outside the return window?

Once an order passes your configured return window, Reflow shows the
customer a clear message instead of a return button. You can still handle
exceptions manually through Shopify if you choose to.

### Can different stores on the same account have different settings?

Each connected store has its own return window, branding, and sidebar
configuration — settings don't carry over between stores.

### Who do I contact for support?

Reach out through the support link in your Reflow admin, or email
support directly — a real person will get back to you.
