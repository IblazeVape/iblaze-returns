---
title: Customization
description: Everything you can configure to make Reflow look and feel like your own store.
---

Reflow is deliberately simple to customize — a handful of settings, all
available from your admin, cover branding, rules, and wording.

## Return window

Set how many days after delivery a customer can start a return. The
default is 30 days. Move the slider anywhere from 1 to 90 days — Reflow
applies the new window immediately to every order in the portal.

## Sidebar menu

The customer-facing sidebar (News & Updates, Returns Policy, Speak to
Support, Back to Store) can be toggled item by item. Turn off anything
you don't want shown, and relink any item to a different URL — your own
FAQ page, a support form, or anywhere else.

## Brand colours

Pick a brand colour and it carries through to the customer avatar and
accent colours across the portal, so it reads as part of your storefront
rather than a bolted-on third-party tool.

## Custom domain

On paid plans, point a subdomain like `returns.yourstore.com` at your
Reflow portal instead of using the default Reflow-hosted URL. Add a CNAME
record with your DNS provider pointing at the address shown in your
Reflow admin, then enter the domain there to activate it.

## Returns policy checkbox

By default, customers must tick "I accept the returns policy" before
submitting a return. You can turn this requirement off entirely, or leave
it on and edit the exact wording to match your own policy.

## Button colours

The Submit, Accept, and Review buttons throughout the portal can be
recoloured to match your storefront's own palette, instead of the default
Reflow styling.
